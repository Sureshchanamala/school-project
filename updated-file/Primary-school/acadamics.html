Curriculum

Affiliated to Council for the Indian School Certificate Examinations, New Delhi since 1980.

ICSE – Indian Certificate of Secondary Education(Classes IX and X)

Subjects Offered

English, Mathematics, Science, Social Studies

II Languages (Telugu / Hindi)

Computer Education – Classes I to VIII

Elective Subjects

Computer Applications / Commercial Applications / Fashion Designing(Classes VIII to X)

SUPW is a compulsory subject.

TESTS AND EXAMINATIONS

Three Unit Tests and Three Terminal Examinations per academic year. Promotion is based on the performance in these examinations.

ACADEMIC INSPECTION CELL

Academic Inspection Cell continuously monitors and evaluates the Academic work in the school and guides the teaching staff in updating and upgrading their teaching process.

Staff

The school is equipped with well trained staff members. The matters concerning the Academics is delegated through the Heads of the Departments, Senior Teachers & Assistant Teachers. For every 2 classes one Floor – Incharge is deputed to look into the administrative and day to day matters relating to their classes. The staff comprises a group of teachers over 30 years of experience who oversee and guide the affairs of academics.
