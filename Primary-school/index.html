St. Joseph’s Public School is a reputed School in Hyderabad. St. Joseph’s Education Society, the brainchild of Sri Udumula Gregory Reddy (U.G. Reddy) established St. Anthony’s High School at Himayathnagar with a State Board curriculum in 1970. Within no time the task of providing quality education which began humbly has grown massively encompassing under its shade thousands of students and hundreds of teachers. Every year the trust of the parents was reinforced with the school setting new levels of standards in the field of education. The demand from parents for this kind of quality education has led to the emergence of St. Joseph’s Public School, affiliated to the Council for Indian School Certificate Examination, New Delhi at King Koti in 1980.

Experienced Teachers

The school is equipped with well trained staff members. The matters concerning the Academics is delegated through the Heads of the Departments, Senior Teachers & Assistant Teachers.

For every 2 classes one Floor – Incharge is deputed to look into the administrative and day to day matters relating to their classes. The staff comprises a group of teachers over 30 years of experience who oversee and guide the affairs of academics.

TESTS AND EXAMINATIONS

_

Three Unit Tests and Three Terminal Examinations per academic year. Promotion is based on the performance in these examinations.

ACADEMIC INSPECTION CELL

_

Academic Inspection Cell continuously monitors and evaluates the Academic work in the school and guides the teaching staff in updating and upgrading their teaching process.

School Facilities:

An effective school facility is aware of the changing programs of educational delivery, and at a minimum should provide a physical environment that’s comfortable, safe, secure, accessible, well illuminated, well ventilated, and aesthetically pleasing.

  • Spacious and well-ventilated classrooms.
  • Smart Boards for Classes P-I, P-II & I to V.
  • Three well equipped computer labs with latest hardware and software.
  • Three well equipped science labs – Physics, Chemistry, Biology.
  • Two well stocked Libraries for Primary & Highschool.
  • An auditorium with a seating capacity of 500 audience.
  • Two basket ball courts, throw ball court, volley ball court and lawn tennis court.
  • The school building is covered with Fire Safety Equipment installed with Fire Safety measures.
  • The building, classrooms and the corridors are under CC Camera surveillance.
  • Water Treatment Plant supplies purified drinking water to all buildings & all floors.
  • A Generator facility is available in the school.
