EDUCATIONAL FACILITIES

SMART CLASSROOM

At ACE we focus on imparting skill based knowledge using variety of learning techniques. We recognize that students at this level are curious, imaginative, experimental, and active and, above all, very  frank. We believe that every child has his or her own unique abilities, interests, aspirations and expectations. Therefore, our curriculum is designed and  structured to encourage students to become self reliant individuals and global citizens as envisaged by the national curriculum framework 2005.

LANGUAGE LAB

The need for computer assisted instruction is paramount in preparing children for the communicative requirement of the competitive world. Hence, the school has built in a language lab where the students are trained on their speaking and listening skills. The language lab console can monitor and guide the activities of each child by means of the software. Apart from scope for improving listening skill, children are led through several modules of speaking tasks based on observation, group discussion, and pair work and topic presentations. Thus, the language learning gets scaffolded further.

LIBRARY

The Library operations are facilitated by means of software that integrates the functioning of three spacious,well-furnished and well stocked libraries with

  1. Reference materials for students to quench their thirst for inquisitiveness
  2. Periodicals that connect them to the dissemination of streaming information
  3. A wide range of literary genre appealing to their taste and
  4. A massive collection of research materials on education in the teachers’ library.

Kids from 1 to 4 can get streamlined to a world of reading through a colourful and illustrated collection of children’s literature kept in the KIDS LIBRARY. Besides the librarian, teachersare assigned the role to ensure that the kids make maximum and effective use of the library periods.

The Main Library is used by students from 5 and above and the teachers Library are a resource hall for the teaching staff to widen their horizon of knowledge and deepen their depth of expertise.

IT LAB

The school provides ample facilities for students to be techno savvy offering computers for every child with internet facilities. The curriculum followed gives a gradual process of content delivery and its periodic assessment. The students are given all facilities in the Lab to do projects, assignments and presentations.

SCIENCE LAB

Laboratories have a key role to play in a child’s learning to make reasonable connection between theory and experience. The physics, chemistry and biology labs of the school have been set up for easier realization of this goal. The students by means of careful observation and analysis of the experiments make meaning of theoretical concepts. The labs are well maintained with all safety standards. They are stocked and updated as per the requirements. Children keep records documenting lab procedures, make oral presentations of their analysis and do practical experiments. Here, they learn the art of cooperation, self-responsibility and communicative skills adding colours to their competence in the modern world.
