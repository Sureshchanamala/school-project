ABOUT ACE PUBLIC SCHOOL

ACE PUBLIC SCHOOL is run by ACE Charitable Trust (ACT), a registered trust formed to spread the light of knowledge and values. ACT is committed to the educational and moral upliftment of the society. The establishment of high quality educational institutions is the prime venture of the trust. The institutions under the banner ACT strive to provide high quality education for all its students in safe, caring, stimulating environment. It endeavors to cater for the moral, social, emotional, physical and academic needs of its students. At ACE we have embarked on a process of continual improvement, to develop and enhance the quality of all aspects of the schools operations.
